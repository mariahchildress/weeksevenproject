My Idea

The idea for this trip came to me in May of last year. I was a tired, burnt out teacher needing a vacation. I decided to go to Disneyland!

I decided that I could make this a quick trip. I would fly out on a Saturday morning, spend Sunday at Disneyland, Monday at Disney's California Adventure, then fly home on Tuesday.

I was able to schedule this so that I could meet up with my parents, who were on a cross country vacation.

This was my plan, and eventually I was able to stick with this:

  1. Fly into LAX on July 10th
  2. Spend the day at Disneyland on July 11th
  3. Mom and dad meet me in Anaheim that night
  4. Spend the day at Disney California Adventure on July 12th
  5. Mom and dad continue on their trip on July 13th and I fly home the same day
